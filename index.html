ZedaTek is the technology knowledge, research, and design vertical of Vidyutra focused on engineering intelligence, software systems, and applied innovation.

About ZedaTek

ZedaTek operates at the intersection of knowledge, research, design, and software. We explore, document, design, and prototype technology systems that can be executed at scale by ZedaWorks or partner organizations.

Our work spans electronics, embedded systems, automation logic, software platforms, and educational technology translating ideas into well-engineered, real-world solutions.

Our Focus Domains

Technology Knowledge

Documentation, blogs, tools, and educational content around electronics, communication systems, and applied engineering.

Research & Development

Exploration of hardware, software, and system architectures through experiments, proof-of-concepts, and technical studies.

Design & Engineering

Electronic design, embedded systems, architecture planning, and design-for-manufacturing collaboration.

Software & Platforms

Software utilities, automation tools, interfaces, and system logic that power modern technology stacks.

What We Create

ZedaTek creates intellectual and technical foundations from design files and software to reference architectures, prototypes, and learning resources.

These outputs are executed, manufactured, and deployed through ZedaWorks or trusted partners, ensuring a clean separation between innovation and production.
